>>Net4Engineers till date ...
        Net4Engineers was launched by Dr.Ashoka Chandra (special secretary,
Ministry of H.R.D. Govt. of India) on 2nd Feb. 2002. The main aim of the
website is to establish itself as a gateway of information for students studying in various engineering colleges. Since then the website has been instrumental in providing a number of services to the students.Within one year Net4engineers has emerged as a resource manager for both students who will take uo engineering as a career and those who will soon be engineers.

Some of the major achievements of the website are:-
>First website in india to show online status of the counselling session 2002 at Kurukshetra university.
>Provide free hosting services for colleges and students.
>First website to provide syllabus and question papers for the curriculum online.
>One stop shop for engineers.

What's up next ?
Continuing it's tradition Net4Engineers, is on it's way to start free online testing services for mock tests on CAT , GATE , IIT-JEE , DCE , CEET exclusively for engineers and to be engineers.
